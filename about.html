Welcome to the British College in Akurana

The British college in Akurana should be the school of first choice for the offering students a broad and balanced education in an environment which compares favourably with the best international standards & it is an exception among English medium primary educational institutes in Sri Lanka where children are provided with fundamentals of education using innovative teaching and learning

Our Milestones

1999-01-01 Where the story begun

The school was opened at Hill Street as “World Vision International School”, lately branch of the London Academy of Speech was also established in the same premises and the school reached its full student enrollment by November.

2000-01-02 The First Concert

The first annual concert is held with 27 students.

2001-01-01 First Achievement in Speech and Drama examination

The first batch of students sat for their first Speech & Drama examination through Victoria College London and all students passed with honours.

2001-01-01 All Island championship in Choral Speaking

Achieving All Island championship in Choral Speaking at the Festival of Music, dance & Speech affiliated to the British Federation of Festivals – UK.

2002-01-01 Victories at Speech examination

The first batch of students sat for Speech examinations conducted by the Thames Valley University – UK and passed with Distinctions.

2002-01-02 Commencement of the Primary section

The primary section commenced with only two students.

2002-01-03 Hosting British Teacher Training Programme

British Teacher Training Programmes are held at the school. Many teachers from elite International & Government schools enrolled in the courses offered with British certification.

2002-01-04 First “Bakthi Gee

The first “Bhakthi Gee” programme was held.

2003-01-01 Reaching full enrolment

The student population of the school reached its full enrollment capacity.

2004-01-01 First Christmas Carol and Graduation

The first Christmas Carols & Graduation were held.

2004-01-02 To a New Place

A new premise opens at Kiribathgoda as the student population increases.

2005-01-01 Advent of Swimming to the school

Swimming was introduced to the students.

2005-01-01 First Activity Day

The first activity day was held.

2006-01-01 Badminton for the First Time

Badminton was introduced to the Primary students.

2007-01-01 Introducing Athletic to the Primary

Athletics was introduced to the Primary students.

2010-01-01 New Name for the School

The management decided to change the name of the school as “British International School”.

2011-01-01 New Swimming pool at Sapugaskande

The school built its own swimming pool at Sapugaskande.

2013-01-01 Moving to Kiribathgoda

The school leased out a new premises in the Housing scheme, Kiribathgoda.

2018-01-01 Finally, at Our Own Space

The school was moved to its own permanent premises.

2018-02-15 Sports Meet Day

2019-01-01 Enrolment of students for primary Section

Students from other school are enrolled into the primary section.

2019-01-02 First Time at an Athletic meet

Our Primary students took part in the Gampaha provincial athletics meet & secured places.

2019-01-02 Changing of School premises

The school moves to Makola road.
